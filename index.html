Damped Oscillator Visualization

Key Concepts

  • Oscillation: Repetitive and periodic motion of a system about an equilibrium position.
  • Damping: Process of reducing the amplitude of oscillations over time, caused by external forces or resistive elements.
  • Natural Frequency: Frequency at which the system would oscillate without damping forces.
  • Damping Ratio (ζ): Dimensionless parameter influencing the rate of oscillation decay.

Mathematical Model

Second-Order Linear Homogeneous Differential Equation

The given differential equation is:

m * d2x/dt2 + c * dx/dt + kx = 0

General Solution

Now, let's express the general solution in terms of these symbols:

x(t) = A * e-βt * cos(ωdt + ϕ)

So, the symbolic representation of the solution is:

x(t) = A * e-(c / (2m))t * cos(sqrt(k / m - (c / (2m))2) * t + ϕ)
